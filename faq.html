Frequently Asked Questions

Your most common questions answered below.

1. How can I place an order?

Go to the product page, choose your size and color, then click add to cart and proceed to checkout.

2. How long does delivery take?

Delivery normally takes 5 to 10 working days depending on your location.

3. Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to many countries including Denmark, Azerbaijan, UAE, UK and more.

4. Can I return a product?

Yes, you can return a product within 7 days if it is unused and in original packaging.

5. How can I contact support?

You can contact us through the Contact Us page or email us at support@asadia.store.
